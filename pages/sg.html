Small Group

Life is never meant to be lived alone. God created us for community and to belong. But, we know that revealing our true selves can be intimidating. Thankfully, at 180 Church, we don't take ourselves too seriously. We recognize that everyone is messed up, and we need others to help us grow. So we encourage you to join one of our small groups and journey in faith together.
